Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 1, 2024

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application for trainers.

1. Information Collection

1.1. We collect personal user data such as names, email addresses, birth dates, and photos if the user selects a client from the address book or manually provides this information.

1.2. We do not collect personal data such as phone numbers or health information of clients.

1.3. We do not collect data automatically. The application does not use cookies or similar technologies for automatic data collection.

1.4. We do not collect data when processing subscription payments. The integrated payment system may collect and process transaction information to prevent fraud and ensure security. For more details, please refer to the payment service provider.

2. Information Use

2.1. Information is stored exclusively on the user's device and is used to manage client records, plan workouts, make and withdraw payments, and generate reports.

2.2. Data is not transferred or sold to third parties.

3. Information Storage

3.1. The information will be stored exclusively on the user's device. The user can download the database and save it wherever they need.

3.2. Files created by the mobile application will be stored until the application is deleted or the files are corrupted for any reason.

4. Information Protection

4.1. Data is stored in the database in plain text. There are no measures to protect against unauthorized access to data on the user's device.

5. User Rights

5.1. The user has the ability to:

  • Add, edit, and delete clients.
  • Add, edit, and delete workouts.
  • Add, edit, and delete training types (data will not be physically deleted but marked as inactive).
  • Add, edit, and delete currencies (data will not be physically deleted but marked as inactive).
  • Top up client balances.
  • Edit transactions.

5.2. All management is carried out through the mobile application. If users wish to delete their data, they need to delete the mobile application and delete all backup copies of data manually created and saved by the users.

6. Contact Information

6.1. In case of questions or complaints regarding privacy, users can contact us via email at web.alex.apps@gmail.com. Please indicate CoachRM in the subject line and write your question or complaint in free form in the body of the email.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

7.1. In case of updates to the privacy policy, users will be able to learn about it from the description of the new version of the mobile application in their system's app store.

7.2. In the event of a privacy policy update, users will see a pop-up message in the updated app.

If you have any questions or comments regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at the provided contact information.
